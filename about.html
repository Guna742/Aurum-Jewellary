About AURUM

Crafting timeless legacies through the artistry of fine jewellery since 1992.

✦ Our Mission

Brilliance in Every Detail

We believe that jewellery is more than an ornament — it is a vessel for memories, a symbol of love, and a testament to human skill. AURUM JEWELLERY was founded on the principle of archival quality and ethical brilliance.

Starting as a small family atelier, we have grown into a global house of design, known for our signature 22K heritage gold and GIA-certified diamond creations. Every piece is handcrafted by master artisans who have dedicated their lives to the bench.

✦ Our Heritage

The AURUM Heritage

1992

The Beginning

Founded as a small family atelier in Florence, dedicated to the art of traditional goldsmithing.

2005

Global Expansion

Established our first international showroom in London, introducing our Heritage Gold collection.

2018

Digital Era

Launched our first bespoke digital concierge service, bringing artisan jewellery to the world.

2026

The Future

Continuing our legacy with sustainable lab-grown options alongside our classic GIA-certified gems.
