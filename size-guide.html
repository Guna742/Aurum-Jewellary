Size Guide

Find your perfect fit with our comprehensive sizing charts for rings, necklaces, and bracelets.

info Getting Started

How to Measure

Accurate measurements ensure the best fit. Follow these simple steps using a soft measuring tape.

1

Ring Diameter

Measure the inside diameter of an existing ring that fits your finger perfectly.

2

Finger Circumference

Wrap a piece of string around your finger and measure the length in millimeters.

3

Wrist Size

Measure your wrist just below the wrist bone where you would normally wear a bracelet.

4

Necklace Drop

Consider where you want the pendant to sit. Standard lengths range from 16 to 24 inches.

Fit Tips

straighten

Between Sizes?

For rings, we recommend sizing up. For necklaces, consider a piece with an adjustable chain or extender.

thermostat

Temperature Matters

Fingers shrink in the cold and swell in the heat. Measure your finger at the end of the day when they are at their largest.

visibility

Bespoke Fitting

For high-jewellery pieces, we offer personalized fitting sessions at our flagship boutiques.

forum

Ask Our Stylists

Not sure about fit? Our personal stylists offer free sizing consultations for all members. Just reach out via chat.

apparel

Designer Sizing Varies

Italian and French designers often run small. Our product pages note any sizing anomalies for each specific piece.

inventory_2

Try Early

We recommend trying your rental as soon as it arrives. If the fit isn't right, contact us immediately for an exchange.
